# Case Study: UPI Payment Fraud
### CSE0001 – Digital Literacy | Raag Shri | 25BAI10431

---

## What is UPI Payment Fraud?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) fraud is a form of cybercrime
in which attackers trick victims into transferring money or
revealing sensitive payment credentials through deception,
impersonation, or fake payment requests.

---

## Realistic Scenario

Riya, a 19-year-old first-year college student from Bhopal,
posted her old laptop for sale on OLX. Within an hour, she
received a message from a buyer named "Vikram" who claimed
to be an Army officer and said he was very interested.

## How It Happened — Step by Step

1. **Contact established** — Vikram messaged Riya on WhatsApp
   saying he would pay the full amount immediately via UPI.

2. **Trust building** — He sent a fake Army ID card and spoke
   very politely and confidently to gain her trust.

3. **The trap** — Vikram said he had already sent ₹25,000
   and asked Riya to "accept" the payment by scanning a
   QR code he sent her.

4. **The deception** — The QR code was actually a
   COLLECT/REQUEST link, not a payment. By entering her
   UPI PIN, Riya authorised money to leave her account,
   not enter it.

5. **Money lost** — ₹18,000 was debited from Riya's account
   instantly. Vikram's number was immediately switched off.

---

## Who is Usually Targeted?

- College students and young adults selling items on
  platforms like OLX and Facebook Marketplace
- First-time UPI users unfamiliar with how QR codes work
- People in urgent need of money who may act quickly
  without verifying

---

## Consequences

- **Financial loss** — Victims lose money that is rarely
  recovered once transferred via UPI
- **Emotional distress** — Feelings of shame, anxiety,
  and loss of trust in digital payments
- **Legal complications** — Filing an FIR and tracing
  the fraudster is a long and uncertain process
- **Academic impact** — For students, losing savings can
  directly affect their ability to manage expenses
